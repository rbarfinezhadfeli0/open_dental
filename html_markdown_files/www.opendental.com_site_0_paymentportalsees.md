			<h1>Payment Portal: What Patient Sees</h1>
		</div>
		<div id="Content">
<p>The Payment Portal allows patients to make online payments with an integrated credit card processor. </p>
<p>In a web browser, launch the Payment Portal.</p>
<img src="images/paymentPortalPatientSelect.png" width="915" height="677"/><p>The Payment Portal uses a unique link specific to the practice. The URL can be embedded on the practice's website, sent via email, or accessed via Patient Portal. </p>
<h2>Pay Now or Verify and Pay</h2>
<p>After entering patient details, patients can make a one-time payment without viewing their balance or can verify their information to view their balance and save their payment method for future use.</p>
<img src="images/paymentPortalPayOption.png" width="915" height="677"/><h2>Pay Now</h2>
<p>If Pay Now is selected, the patient is taken to the payment amount screen.</p>
<img src="images/paymentPortalHowMuch.png" width="915" height="677"/><p>From here, patients can enter their card number and continue with payment. Screens and fields may vary depending on the payment processor. </p>
<img src="images/paymentPortalCardInfo.png" width="915" height="677"/><h2>Verify and Pay</h2>
<p>If Verify and Pay is selected, the patient is sent a verification code to the patient information on file. </p>
<img src="images/paymentPortalVerificationCode.png" width="915" height="677"/><p>They can then choose to pay their balance, or a specific amount.</p>
<img src="images/paymentPortalAmount.png" width="915" height="677"/><p>Next, the patient can select from an existing card, add a new card, or delete cards no longer in use. For PaySimple users, a bank account can be used as well.</p>
<img src="images/paymentPortalChoose.png" width="915" height="677"/><h2>Receipt</h2>
<p>The result of the payment (e.g., successful, unsuccessful) will display. The patient will receive an emailed receipt, or they can print a receipt from this window.</p>
<img src="images/paymentPortalReceipt.png" width="915" height="677"/>
