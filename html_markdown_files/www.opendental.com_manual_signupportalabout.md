<p><b>Legacy Type</b>: Historical billing information.</p>
<p><b>Listener Type</b>: Indicates what the eConnector is doing.</p>
<p><b>Permission Level</b>: The permission level of the logged on user. Those with Full Access (<i>Security Admin</i> permission) can change eServices. Those without have read-only access.</p>
<p><b>Is Reseller Customer</b>?: Indicates if there is a reseller registration key.</p>
