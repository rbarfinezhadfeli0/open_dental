<p>Saved treatment plans can be sent to eClipboard for patients to review and sign.</p>
<p>In the <a href="treatmentplan.html">Treatment Plan Module</a>, highlight a saved treatment plan. Click the <b>eClipboard</b> button. </p>

<h2>Patient Currently in Session</h2>
<p>If a patient is currently in an eClipboard session, highlight the Saved Treatment Plan then click <b>eClipboard</b>.</p>
<p>The Treatment Plan will be added to their Check-in checklist.</p>
<img src="images/eClipboardCheckInTP.png" width="700" height="318"/><h2>Patient Not in Session</h2>
<p>If the patient is not currently in an eClipboard session, the treatment plan can be sent to the device. </p>
<p class="MarginBottomZero"></p>
<ol class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>In the Treatment Plan Module, highlight the Saved Treatment Plan, then click <b>eClipboard</b>. <br/></li>
<li>The following window will show.<br/><img src="images/eClipboardTPQR.png" width="328" height="331" class="ImageInParagraph"/></li>
<li>In eClipoard, click the QR icon.<br/><img src="images/eClipboardQRButton.png" width="700" height="95" class="ImageInParagraph"/></li>
<li>Either use the device camera to scan the QR code, or type in the unlock code. </li>
<li>The treatment plan will populate in eClipboard.<br/><img src="images/eclipboardTPUnlocked.png" width="700" height="917" class="ImageInParagraph"/><ul>
<li>Click the Full Screen button in the upper-right to zoom in on the treatment plan. </li>
<li>Additional saved treatment plans can be sent to the device for the patient to compare and review.</li>
<li>Once a patient has made a decision, click <b>Sign</b>. The signature field will display to sign electronically.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Saving Treatment Plans </h2>
<p> Before treatment plans signed via eClipboard can be saved, <a href="https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=52685">Microsoft Visual C++ 2015 Redistributable Update 3</a> must be installed on the server. </p>
<p>Treatment plans signed via eClipboard are automatically saved to the Imaging Module if the preference, <i> Save signed Treatment Plans to PDF</i>, is enabled, and the OpenDentImages folder is using network file storage, Dropbox, or SFTP (cannot be a local folder).</p>
<h2>Updating Treatment Plans</h2>
<p>Treatment plans can be updated while still loaded to the eClipboard device. Simply make the edits to the saved treatment plan in Open Dental, then click <b>eClipboard</b>. Updates will reflect in eClipboard. </p>
<p>To remove a treatment plan currently loaded to eClipboard, in the patient's Treatment Plan module, click the <i>X </i>next to the saved treatment plan in the Treatment Plans grid.</p>
