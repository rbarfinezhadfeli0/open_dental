<p>Version 20.2 was released on 07/20/2020.</p>
<p>This version is notably smaller than normal. Most of 20.2 was developed during COVID-19. As a result, our team focused more on larger features that would benefit our customers during this time.</p>

<li>Open Dental Mobile. <a href="../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl=%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7opendentalmobile.html">ODMobile</a> (Also available in 20.1)</li>
<li>Arrival messages makes it easier to check patients in from their car. <a href="../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl=%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7arrivalssetup.html">Arrivals</a> (Also available in 20.1)</li>
</ul>

<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
