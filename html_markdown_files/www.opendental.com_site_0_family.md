<h2>Family Toolbar</h2>
<img src="images/familyToolbar.png" width="915" height="30"/><p><b>Family Members</b>: Add and delete patients, set a family member as the guarantor, and move a patient to a different family.</p>
<p><b>Clones</b>: Create and manage <a href="0_clone.html">Patient Clones</a>.</p>
<p><b>Super Family</b>: Create and manage a <a href="0_superfamily.html">Super Family</a>. This feature is used to group multiple families into an overarching group. This is helpful for billing.</p>
<p><b>Add Insurance</b>: Attach an insurance plan or view all insurance plans for the family. </p>
<p><b>Discount Plan</b>: Attach a <a href="0_discountplan.html">Discount Plan</a> to the patient. This feature can be used to automatically apply treatment discounts for patients without insurance.</p>
<h2>Patient Picture &amp; Family Member List</h2>
<img src="images/familyPatientPic.png" width="590" height="108"/><p>View the patient's photo and quickly switch between family members. </p>
<h2>Recall</h2>
<img src="images/familyRecall.png" width="545" height="108"/><p>View the patient's <a href="0_recall.html">Recall</a> information, including due dates and scheduled dates.</p>
<h2>Patient Information</h2>
<p>A summary of patient information, including contact information. Open the <a href="0_patientedit.html">Patient Information</a> by double-clicking the area to edit information. Available fields in this area are customizable.</p>
<img src="images/familyPatInfo.png" width="260" height="397"/><p>This area also lists referrals and <a href="carecredit.html">CareCredit</a> information.</p>
<h2>Insurance Plans</h2>
<p>The Insurance Plans area displays all insurance plans currently attached to the patient as active coverage. A nearly unlimited amount of plans can be attached (primary, secondary, tertiary, primary medical, secondary medical, etc.) Below the plan details is the insurance history area. </p>
<img src="images/familyInsArea.png" width="524" height="397"/><h2>Discount Plans</h2>
<p>This area replaces the Insurance Plans area when a <a href="0_discountplan.html">Discount Plan</a> has been attached to the patient. </p>
