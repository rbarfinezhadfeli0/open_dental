<p>Released 1/23/2008 </p>
<p><b>Appointment module:</b> Multiple appointments may be placed on the pinboard.</p>
<p><b>Recall:</b> Schedule for entire family at once. <a href="../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl=%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7apptspatient.html">Patient Appointments</a>. </p>
<p><b>Inventory Management:</b> Track supplies needed, organize suppliers, organize supplies by supplier and category, organize orders in progress, keep history of orders, and print lists. <a href="../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl=%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7supplyinventory.html">Supply Inventory</a>. </p>
<p><b>Email pdf's:</b> Treatment plans and statements may both be sent by email as pdf's with one click.</p>
<p><b>Task lists: </b>Users may now 'subscribe' to certain lists. This gives them a central place to manage all their tasks. Task lists that have incomplete tasks within them now turn orange and show a number. When logging in, users are notified if they have any incomplete tasks. <a href="../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl=%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7tasklists.html">Tasklists</a>. </p>
<p> Sign each TP digitally. <a href="../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl=%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7treatmentplan.html">Treatment Plan</a> module. <br>  Planned appointment tracker and Unscheduled list: Provider selection. Sort by status, alph, or date. <br>  Popups can be disabled for custom amounts of time.<br>  Print Planned Appt Tracker list.<br> Option to show the ! again at the upper right of appointments for procedures not attached to claims. <br> Option to hide the discount column in the TP module. <br> Provider income transfer window no longer shows by default after entering an insurance payment. <br> Send claims window: Filter by clinic.<br> Send claims window: Drop down list to pick clearinghouse to send to. Useful when there are multiple clinics, each with their own clearinghouse account. <br> Database maint: Fixed situation where user changed status of proc to TP, but it was still attached to claim. </p>
<p>Please see <a href="../manual/claimtabattach.html">Electronic Attachments</a> for some recent changes regarding this feature. <br></p>
