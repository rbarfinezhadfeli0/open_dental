<div class="GeneralPageContent">
<p>Below is an explanation of how each field on the printed ADA 2012, 2018, 2019, and 2024 <a href="claimforms.html">Claim Forms</a> are populated.</p>
<div class="Note">Note: <ul>
<li>The requirements for e-claims are different than the requirements for paper claims. Printing a claim does not represent what is sent in an e-claim. Likewise, information sent in an e-claim does not necessarily print on a paper claim.</li>
<li>The 2012 and 2018/2019 claims forms are nearly identical. The few changes are noted in their respective fields.</li>
<li>The 2024 claim form added new fields. These new fields are noted in their respective fields.</li>
<li>Also see <a href="claimsaddresses.html">Claim Addresses</a> for details on entering address and phone information.</li>
</ul>
