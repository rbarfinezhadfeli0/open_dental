<p>The medical area is where staff can imput important patient medical information like current medication, problems, allergies, etc.</p>
<p>In the <a href="0_chart.html">Chart Module</a>, double-click the pink Medical area. Click the Medical Info tab.</p>
<img src="images/medical.png" width="804" height="521"/><h2>Medical Info Tab</h2>
<p class="MarginBottomZero">Use the Medical Info tab (shown above) to record general medical information about the patient. </p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li><b>Premedicate</b>: Easily indicate if a patient needs to be premedicated. This flag can also be displayed on appointments for quick reference.</li>
<li><b>Med Urgent</b>: Urgent medical notes about the patient. This information can be displayed on appointments for quick reference.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Problems Tab</h2><p>From the Problems tab, you can track any medical conditions or problems a patient may have.</p>
<img src="images/medicalProblems.png" width="804" height="257"/><p>Problems are selected from a customizable master list.</p>
<h2>Medications Tab</h2><p>From the Medications tab, add medications the patient is taking. </p>
<img src="images/medicalMedications.png" width="804" height="257"/><p>Medications are automatically added when creating a prescription in Open Dental or when using <a href="electronicrx.html">eRx</a>. Other medications (e.g., over-the-counter or prescriptions from other providers) can be added to the patient from a customizable master list.</p>
<h2>Allergies Tab</h2><p>Track patient allergies from the Allergies tab.</p>
<img src="images/medicalAllergies.png" width="804" height="257"/><p>Allergies are selected from a customizable master list.</p>
<h2>Vital Signs</h2><p>Track patient vital signs (pulse, blood pressure, etc.) that are recorded during a visit.</p>
<img src="images/medicalVitalSigns.png" width="804" height="295"/><h2>Medical Information in the Chart</h2><p>Medical information is displayed in the Patient Info area of the <a href="0_chart.html">Chart Module</a>.</p>
<img src="images/medicalPanel.png" width="419" height="253"/><p>The displayed fields and background color of the medical area are customizable. </p>
