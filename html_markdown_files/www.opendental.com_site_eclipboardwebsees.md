<p>On a web browser, go to the <a href="eclipboard.html">eClipboard</a> Web URL.</p>
<p>eClipboard Web allows patients to fill forms online from any web browser. It is nearly identical to eClipboard 25 but does not allow for patient check-in, nor does the practice need to enter office login credentials. </p>
<p>eClipboard Web is an updated alternative solution to Web Forms. </p>
<h2>Patient Login</h2>
<img src="images/eclipboardWebLogin.png" width="915" height="663"/><p>Patients log in using their first name, last name, and date of birth. </p>
<p>To verify their identity, they must also enter the phone number or email address associated with their patient account in Open Dental. A code is immediately sent to the entered selection. </p>
<h2>Patient Verification</h2>
<img src="images/eClipboardWebVerify.png" width="915" height="664"/><p>After the patient enters the Verification code, they are directed to the checklist to complete needed items.</p>
<p>For a look at other eClipboard examples, such as the check-in list, filling eForms, and more, see: <a href="../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl=%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7eclipboard25sees.html">eClipboard 25: What Patient Sees</a>.</p>
