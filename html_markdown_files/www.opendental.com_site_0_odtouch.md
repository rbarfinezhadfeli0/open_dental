		<div id="Content">
<p>Use ODTouch to access appointment actions for a patient including treatment plans, charting, and more.</p>
<p>In ODTouch, the Home Screen opens upon login.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchHome.png" width="766" height="508"/><p>In the Home Screen, you can interact with daily appointments, select patients, view provider goals, and go through appointment actions for the selected patient.</p>
<h2>Treatment Plans</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Tx Plans.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchTxPlans.png" width="771" height="512"/><p>You can create, edit, and save <a href="0_treatmentplan.html">Treatment Plans</a> to print or have the patient sign in the app. </p>
<h2>Perio Charts</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Perio.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchPerio.png" width="772" height="505"/><p>Using this screen, you can create <a href="0_periochart.html">Perio Exams</a>, use voice perio charting, and view summary and graphical perio charts.</p>
<h2>Payment Plans</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Pay Plans.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchPayPlan.png" width="774" height="512"/><p>Create or modify <a href="0_paymentplan.html">Payment Plans</a> from scratch or by using a template, then print the payment plan or have the patient sign it in the app.</p>
<h2>Images</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Images.</p>
<img src="images/odTouchImages.png" width="767" height="508"/><p>View images from the <a href="0_images.html">Imaging Module</a>, capture new images, or edit existing image information.</p>
<h2>Exam Sheets</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Exam Sheet.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchExam.png" width="773" height="513"/><p>You can create custom <a href="0_examsheet.html">Exam Sheets</a> that can also be filled out in the app.</p>
<h2>eRouting</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap eRouting.</p>
<img src="images/odtoucheRouting.png" width="766" height="508"/><p>Using eRouting, you can create custom checklists of appointment actions to take for patients.</p>
<h2>Medical Information</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Medical.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchMedicalInfo.png" width="771" height="501"/><p>From this screen, you can add <a href="0_medical.html">Medical Information</a> for your patient such as any notes, problems, medications, or allergies the patient has. Vital signs can also be tracked here.</p>
<h2>eRx</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap eRx.</p>
<img src="images/odtoucheRx.png" width="769" height="502"/><p>You can use the <a href="dosespot.html">DoseSpot eRx Feature</a> to create and send prescriptions directly from the app.</p>
<h2>Progress Notes and Charting Treatment</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Chart.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchChart.png" width="766" height="508"/><p>From this screen, you can interact with the progress notes from the <a href="0_chart.html">Chart Module</a> as well as create prescriptions to print and consent forms for the patient to sign.</p>
<h3>Charting Procedures</h3><p>In ODTouch: Chart, in the bottom right, tap the edit icon.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchChartEdit.png" width="766" height="508"/><p>Using the Chart Edit screen, you can mark teeth as missing/hidden, change the dentition, or chart procedures by using procedure buttons or entering the procedure codes.</p>
<h2>Appointments</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Appts.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchAppts.png" width="769" height="501"/><p>Easily create and <a href="0_apptedit.html">Edit Appointments</a> found in the <a href="0_appointments.html">Appointments Module</a>, including adding procedures, <a href="0_labcases.html">Lab Cases</a>, and other details.</p>
<h2>Reports</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Reports.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchReports.png" width="770" height="501"/><p>You can run basic reports to get a quick look at production, income, and more.</p>
<h2>Integrated Texting</h2><p>From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Messages.</p>
<img src="images/odtouchMessages.png" width="766" height="508"/><p>Using the <a href="textintegrated.html">Integrated Texting Feature</a>, send and receive text messages from patients in the app.</p>
