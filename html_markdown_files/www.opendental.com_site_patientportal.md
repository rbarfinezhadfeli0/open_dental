		<div id="Content">
<p>The Patient Portal is an eService hosted by Open Dental and gives patients instant access to their health information. It also allows providers and patients to exchange private, <a href="securewebmail.html">Secure WebMail</a> messages.</p>
<div align="center">
<img src="images/patientPortalGraphic.png" width="900" height="300"/></div>
<h2>Feature Highlights</h2>
<p><img src="images/youtubeLogo.png" width="16" height="16" style="margin-bottom: 0px"/> Watch our video: <a href="https://youtu.be/R7KcUrY2X3o">A Quick Look at Patient Portal</a>. </p>
<p class="MarginBottomZero">Patient Portal allows patients to do the following: </p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>View statements and make payments</li>
<li>See upcoming appointments</li>
<li>Review Treatment Plans</li>
<li>Send and receive secure <a href="securewebmail.html">WebMail</a></li>
<li>View images and PDFs</li>
<li>And more!</li>
</ul>
<p><a href="secureemail.html">Secure Email</a> users can also use Patient Portal to send and receive Secure Email.</p>
<p>Visit <a href="0_portalpatientsees.html">Patient Portal: What Patient Sees</a> to take a closer look.</p>
<h2>Try out Patient Portal</h2>
<p class="MarginBottomZero">Want to try out Patient Portal before setting it up in your own database? Check out our <a href="https://www.patientviewer.com/pdemo.html">Patient Portal Demo</a>. </p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li><b>User Name</b>: <i>demo</i></li>
<li><b>Password</b>: Any text or no text can be entered</li>
</ul>
<h2>Getting Started</h2>
<p>Patient Portal is available at no additional fee for practices on support with a unique, active registration key.</p>
<p>For a list of supported browsers see <a href="computerrequirements.html">Computer Requirements</a>.</p>
<p>Once you're ready, your office can select which Patient Portal Features are enabled. You can also determine which patients get invites to or access to Patient Portal. </p>
<p>Your office also has the option to host your own Patient Portal. This is a highly technical process and Open Dental does not provide support on this setup. </p>
