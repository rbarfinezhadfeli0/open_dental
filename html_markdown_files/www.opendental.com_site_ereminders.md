		<div id="Content">
<p>Automated Messaging includes eReminders, eConfirmations, Automated Thank-You Messages, Arrivals, General Messages, and Patient Portal Invites. Automated Messaging helps you manage and automate your appointment process. </p>
<div align="center">
<img src="images/autoMsgHeader.png" width="900" height="300"/></div>
<p><img src="images/youtubeLogo.png" width="16" height="16" style="margin-bottom: 0px"/> Watch our video: <a href="https://youtu.be/YuKNAHdNC9g">Automated Messaging</a>. </p>
<h2>Getting Started</h2>
<p>There are multiple Automated Messaging eServices. Practices must be on support and have a unique, active registration key to sign up for Automated Messaging eServices. Practices must remain on support while using the service.</p>
<a name='ereminders'></a><h2>eReminders</h2>
<p class="MarginBottomZero"><b>Feature Highlights</b></p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>Reminders are automatically sent via text and/or email for upcoming appointments</li>
<li>Help patients to remember their appointments by sending reminders shortly before the appointment date</li>
<li>Messages are logged in the <a href="../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl=%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7commlog.html">Commlog</a> for easy tracking</li>
<li>Easily customize message text and lead times to suit your practice needs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Getting Started</b><br> eReminders can be sent via email at no additional cost. To send via text message, <a href="textintegrated.html">Integrated Texting</a> is required. See <a href="fees.html">Fees for Support and Services</a> for pricing.</p>
<a name='econfirmations'></a><h2>eConfirmations</h2>
<p class="MarginBottomZero"><b>Feature Highlights</b></p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>Confirmation messages are automatically sent via text and/or email and allows patients to confirm</li>
<li>Messages are logged in the <a href="0_commlog.html">Commlog</a> for easy tracking</li>
<li>Once the patient replies to the text with the confirmation code or confirms via the URL in the confirmation email, the appointment is automatically marked confirmed in Open Dental</li>
<li>Easily customize message text and lead times to suit your practice needs</li>
<li>Includes New Patient Thank-You service (see below)</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Getting Started</b><br> eConfirmations are included with the <a href="eservicesbundle.html">eServices Bundle</a> or are available for a standalone monthly fee. See <a href="fees.html">Fees for Support and Services</a> for pricing.</p>
<a name='arrivals'></a><h2>Arrivals</h2>
<p class="MarginBottomZero"><b>Feature Highlights</b></p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>A text message is automatically sent to patients at a specified time a message requesting they wait in their vehicle before their appointment. </li>
<li>When a patient arrives for their appointment, they respond to the text and their appointment status is automatically changed to arrived and a text is automatically sent asking them to wait outside.</li>
<li>An Alert is generated in Open Dental to notify staff the patient has arrived.</li>
<li>Staff can easily right-click the appointment to send a <i>Come In</i> text to the patient once they're ready. </li>
</ul>
<p><b>Getting Started</b><br> Arrivals are included with the <a href="eservicesbundle.html">eServices Bundle</a> or require the standalone eConfirmations eService. See <a href="fees.html">Fees for Support and Services</a> for pricing.</p>
<a name='thankyou'></a><h2>Automated Thank-You</h2>
<p class="MarginBottomZero"><b>Feature Highlights</b></p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>A Thank-You Message is automatically sent via text and/or email when a patient schedules an appointment</li>
<li>Patients can save the appointment to the calendar on their device from the message</li>
<li>Messages are logged in the <a href="0_commlog.html">Commlog</a> for easy tracking</li>
<li>Easily customize message text and lead times to suit your practice needs</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Getting Started</b><br> Automated Thank-You messages can be sent via email at no additional cost. To send via text message, <a href="textintegrated.html">Integrated Texting</a> is required. See <a href="fees.html">Fees for Support and Services</a> for pricing.</p>
<a name='npthankyou'></a><h2>New Patient Thank-You</h2>
<p class="MarginBottomZero"><b>Feature Highlights</b></p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>A New Patient Thank-You Message is automatically sent to new patients when a patient schedules their first appointment</li>
<li>A customizable URL is sent in the message for patients to fill out patient forms online via <a href="webforms.html">Web Forms</a></li>
</ul>
<p><b>Getting Started</b><br> New Patient Thank-You messages are included when signed up for the standalone eConfirmations service or the eServices bundle.</p>
<a name='general'></a><h2>General Messages</h2>
<p class="MarginBottomZero"><b>Feature Highlights</b></p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>A message is automatically sent via text and/or email after a patient's appointment has been set complete</li>
<li>Messages are completely customizable and can be used to thank patients for coming to their appointment, invite them to leave a review, etc.</li>
<li>Messages are logged in the <a href="0_commlog.html">Commlog</a> for easy tracking</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Getting Started</b><br> General Messages can be sent via email at no additional cost. To send via text message, <a href="textintegrated.html">Integrated Texting</a> is required. See <a href="https://opendental.com/site/fees.html#eservices">Fees for Support and Services</a> for pricing.</p>
<a name='portalinvite'></a><h2>Patient Portal Invites</h2>
<p class="MarginBottomZero"><b>Feature Highlights</b></p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>If <a href="../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl=%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7portalhostedbyod.html">Patient Portal</a> and Patient Portal Invites are set up, Open Dental sends an email message automatically before or after the appointment, inviting the patient to utilize Patient Portal.</li>
<li>Messages are completely customizable and includes a link to Patient Portal, as well as the patient's username and password. </li>
</ul>
<p><b>Getting Started</b><br> Patient Portal Invites can be sent via email at no additional cost. Patient Portal is required.</p>
