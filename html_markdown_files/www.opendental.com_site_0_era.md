<p>ERA 835, also known as Electronic Remittance Advice format, is an electronic explanation of benefits (EOB) that can be electronically downloaded.</p>
<p>In the <a href="0_manage.html">Manage Module</a>, click ERAs.</p>
<img src="images/claimERA.png" width="915" height="381"/><p><img src="images/YouTube_PlayButton_15px.png" width="16" height="16" style="margin-bottom: 0px"/> See our video: <a href="https://youtu.be/y2BIYuYiRoY">Processing ERAs</a>.</p>
<p class="MarginBottomZero">Automatic download of ERAs is supported by the following clearinghouses: </p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>DentalXChange ClaimConnect E-Claims</li>
<li>Electronic Dental Services</li>
<li>Denti-Cal</li>
</ul>
<p>ERAs from other clearinghouses or carriers can be manually downloaded to Open Dental.</p>
<p>Downloaded ERAs can be used to auto-populate insurance paid amounts into claims used to finalize insurance payments.</p>
<h2>Processing ERAs</h2><p>In the ERAs window, double-click on an ERA.</p>
<img src="images/eraFlowClaims.png" width="915" height="574"/><p>ERAs can be automatically or manually processed.</p>
<h3>Automatic ERA Processing</h3><p>There are two options for autoamtic ERA processing, depending on how your practice wishes to review the ERAs. Fully-Automatic ERA Processing automatically matches ERAs to a claim and finalizes payment. Semi-Automatic ERA Processing allows you to preview ERA information before it is finalized and associated claims can be finalized with a single click. Automatically processed ERAs are included in the ERA 835s list and can be reviewed or updated as needed.</p>
<h3>Manually processing ERA</h3><p>When manually processing ERAs, you will review each claim and payment amounts before manually finalizing payment.</p>
