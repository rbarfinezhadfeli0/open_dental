			<h1>Native Payment Integrations</h1>
		</div>
		<div id="Content">
<p>Open Dental is integrated with three merchant service companies to process payments directly through Open Dental.</p>
<img src="images/headerPaymentIntegrations.png" width="900" height="300"/><p>Open Dental works with vendors to develop and support Native Payment Integrations. </p>
<p class="MarginBottomZero">With a Native Payment Integration: </p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>Payments can be processed directly through Open Dental</li>
<li>Payment details (e.g., credit card numbers or bank account information) can be saved to Open Dental to process future payments or recurring charges.</li>
<li>Payments are automically posted to the patient account in Open Dental</li>
</ul>
<h2>Integrated Options</h2><p>Open Dental has native integrations with three payment processors. Prior to using an integrated payment method, your practice must first sign up with the merchant and enable the program link. </p>
<p>More than one credit card processing program may be enabled in Open Dental and clinics may set up different credentials when using different merchant accounts.</p>
<h3>Global Payments</h3><p>Formerly EdgeExpress (OpenEdge)</p>
<p class="MarginBottomZero"></p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>Available internationally, including Canada.</li>
<li>Supports EMV transactions.</li>
<li>Supports online patient payments through the <a href="paymentportal.html">Payment Portal</a>.</li>
<li>Supports Recurring Charges.</li>
</ul>
<p>See <a href="globalpaymentsintegration.html">Global Payments</a> for more information.<br> Website: <a href="https://www.globalpaymentsintegrated.com/">globalpaymentsintegrated.com</a></p>
<h3>PayConnect by DentalXChange</h3><p></p>
<p class="MarginBottomZero"></p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>Available only in the United States.</li>
<li>Supports EMV transactions.</li>
<li>Supports online patient payments through the <a href="paymentportal.html">Payment Portal</a>.</li>
<li>Supports Recurring Charges.</li>
</ul>
<p> See <a href="dentalxchange.html#payconnect">PayConnect by DentalXChange</a> for more information.<br> Website: <a href="http://www.dentalxchange.com/">dentalxchange.com</a></p>
<h3>PaySimple</h3><p class="MarginBottomZero"><a href="paysimpleintegration.html">PaySimple</a>: Available only in the United States. </p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>Supports swiped credit card transactions plus ACH and direct debit payments from a patient's checking or savings account.</li>
<li>Supports online patient payments through the <a href="paymentportal.html">Payment Portal</a>.</li>
<li>Supports Recurring Charges.</li>
</ul>
<p>See <a href="../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl=%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7paysimple.html">PaySimple</a> for more information.<br> Website: <a href="https://paysimple.com/partner/open-dental">paysimple.com</a></p>
<h3>Additional Information</h3><p>Other merchant services may utilize Open Dental's API to develop their own integration. Open Dental is not directly involved in the development or support of these integrations. See <a href="vendorsthirdparty.html">Third-Party Vendors</a> for more details.</p>
