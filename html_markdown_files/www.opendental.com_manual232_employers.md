<p>The Employer list includes all employers entered for patients in Edit Patient Information and Insurance Plans.</p>
<p>In the <a href="mainmenu.html">Main Menu</a>, click Lists, Employers.</p>
<img src="images/employers.png" width="449" height="480"/><p>This list is automatically updated when a new employer is entered from <a href="patientedit.html">Edit Patient Information</a> or <a href="insplan.html">Insurance Plan</a>. </p>
<p><b>Add</b>: Create a new employer.</p>
<p><b>Delete</b>: Highlight an employer from the list and click to remove it. </p>
<p><b>Edit</b>: Highlight an empoloyer from the list and click to make changes.</p>
<p><b>Combine</b>: See Combine Employers section below.</p>
<p>Click <b>OK</b> to close the window.</p>
<h2>Edit Employer</h2>
<p>Click <b>Add</b> to create a new employer. Double-click an existing employer or highlight an existing employer and click <b>Edit</b> to make changes.</p>
<img src="images/employerEdit.png" width="351" height="185"/><p>Enter or edit the Employer name.</p>
<p>Click <b>Save </b>to complete changes..</p>
<h2>Combine Employers</h2>
<p class="MarginBottomZero">If multiple entries exist for the same employer, they can be combined into a single record.  </p>
<ol class="MarginBottomGap">
<li>Highlight the employers to combine. </li>
<li>Click <b>Combine</b>. <br/><img src="images/employerCombine.png" width="409" height="146" class="ImageInParagraph"/></li>
<li>Click <b>OK </b>to proceed.</li>
</ol>
<p>All combined employers are merged into the employer selected at top. All patient and insurance records are updated to match the employer that remains in the list. Edit the employer as necessary. </p>
