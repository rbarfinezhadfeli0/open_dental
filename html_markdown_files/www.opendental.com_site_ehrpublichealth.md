<p class="MarginBottomZero"><span style="color:red">This information applies to EHR Modified Stage 2 for Program Years 2015 to 2018. For current information, see:</span></p>
<ul class="MarginBottomGap">
<li><a href="ehrmodified2.html">EHR Modified Stage 2</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.cms.gov/Regulations-and-Guidance/Legislation/EHRIncentivePrograms/2019ProgramRequirementsMedicaid.html">2019 Program Requirements - Medicaid</a></li>
<li><a href="https://opendental.com/site/ehrlicense.html">Open Dental EHR</a></li>
</ul>
<p>The EP is in active engagement with a public health agency to submit electronic public health data from the CEHRT, except where prohibited and in accordance with applicable law and practice.</p>
<p><b>Measure Option 1 - Immunization Registry Reporting</b> Provider is in active engagement with a public health agency to submit immunization data.</p>
<p><b>Exclusion?</b></p>
<p>Yes, if provider doesn't administer vaccinations or operates in a jurisdiction that doesn't accept immunization data from EP.</p>
<p><b>Measure Option 2 - Syndromic Surveillance Reporting</b> Provider is in active engagement with a public health agency to submit syndromic surveillance data.</p>
<p>Exclusion? Yes, if provider is in a category of providers from which syndromic surveillance data is not collected or operates in a jurisdiction in which no public health agency is capable of or ready to receive syndromic surveillance data.</p>
<p><b>Measure Option 3 - Specialized Registry Reporting</b> Provider is in active engagement to submit data to a specialized registry.</p>
<p><b>Exclusion?</b></p>
<p>Yes, if provider does not diagnose or treat any disease or condition associated with a specialized registry, or operates in a jurisdiction where no specialized registry is capable of or ready to receive electronic registry data.</p>
<div class="Note">Note: It is possible that dentists will meet exclusion criteria for all public health measures. Even if you intend to claim an exclusion, you may need to sign participation agreements or addendum's. For a full list of public health agencies by state, see <a href="http://www.aphl.org/programs/informatics/Pages/MU-Public-Health-Readiness.aspx">http://www.aphl.org/programs/informatics/Pages/MU-Public-Health-Readiness.aspx</a>.</div>
<p>Contact your state early to determine requirements, sign legal agreements, or begin the onboarding process.</p>
