<p>All exams are listed in this area and up to six of the most recent exams are displayed for comparison. </p>

<h2>Perio Chart</h2>
<p>Data for the currently selected exam is shown in dark text. Data from up to five earlier exams is also displayed. Earlier exams are grayed out.</p>
<img src="images/perioExamChart.png" width="631" height="691"/><p>Enter data for probing depths, Mucogingival Junction, ginigival margin, furcation, and mobility. Clinical Attachment Loss is automatically calculated. </p>
<h2>Entering Data</h2>
<p>Enter data using the on-screen buttons, keyboard shortcuts, built-in voice perio, or Bola AI integration. </p>
