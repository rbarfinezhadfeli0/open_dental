<p>Mass Email is an <a href="eservices.html">eService</a> that allows users to send Mass Email from Open Dental using Mailchimp.</p>

<p>Mass Email through Mailchimp allows users to easily create and send targeted email campaigns to patients. By integrating with Mailchimp, additional analytics are available to view the success of a campaign and manage the audience list. <h2>Feature Highlights</h2><p class="MarginBottomZero"></p>

<li>Automatically commlog email campaigns on patient accounts</li>
</ul>
<h2>Getting Started</h2><p>Mass Email is enabled via the eServices Signup Portal. The fee charged through Open Dental is an access fee. The access fee is only charged for months where emails have been uploaded to Mailchimp or when an email has been sent. See <a href="fees.html">Fees for Software, Support, and Services</a> for access pricing. Additional fees are handled directly in your Mailchimp account. </p>
<p>Practices must be on support and have a unique, active registration key to sign up for Mass Email and must remain on support while using the service.</p>
