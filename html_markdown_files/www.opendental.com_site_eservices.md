<p> eServices are internet-based features available through Open Dental that can provide enhanced access for patients and providers, and automate many tasks. </p>
<p><div align="center">
<img src="images/headereServices.png" width="900" height="300"/></div>
<p>The result is increased productivity and efficiency and a better patient experience, which translates into improved patient retention and practice growth.</p>

<img src="images/eServicesDevicesLarge.png" width="400" height="224" width="400" height="224" align="right"/><h3>eService Fees</h3><p>See <a href="https://opendental.com/site/fees.html#eservices">Fees for Software, Support, and Services</a>.<br>  To sign up, each location must be on support and have a unique, active registration key. Only customers on support will be able to sign up for any eService. Integrated Texting can still be utilized even if the office cancels support; in these cases, we will only provide support limited to the eService still in use. Practices must remain on support while using all other eServices.</p>

<li>Distributors who would like to resell Open Dental eServices, see <a href="distributors.html">Distributors</a>.</li>
