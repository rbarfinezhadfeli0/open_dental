ODTouch: Rx Manage

Manage prescriptions for a patient from the ODTouch app.

In ODTouch: Chart, tap the caret dropdown. Tap Manage Rx.

Existing prescriptions for the patient are listed on the left. Tap a prescription to view details on the right. Information can be edited and saved as needed.

Tap the caret at the top to print the selected prescription.

Tap + to create a new prescription for the patient. See ODTouch: New Rx.
