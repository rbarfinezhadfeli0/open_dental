Super Family

In the Family Module, super families can be used to group patients that are related in some way.

Super Families can be useful for various scenarios, inlcuding:

  • Patients in a particular care facility (e.g., nursing home or retirement community).
  • A split family due to divorce.
  • Members of an athletic team getting mouthguards.

The Super Family feature can be enabled as needed.

In the Family Module, easily create Super Family groups and add existing families or patients to the group. You can easily switch between families in the group.

In the Account Module, switch between families in the Super Family group to view transactions. Statements can be created for individual patients, a single family, or the entire group.
