Help Feature

Starting with Version 24.4, the manual is not available on the website. Potential customers can use the Quick Manual. Full Help pages are only available from within Open Dental to customers on support.

In Open Dental, at the top of any window, click the ? icon.

When clicked, the corresponding help page opens.

If a help page has FAQs included, they appear at the bottom of the page. Click a question to expand it.

Help can also be accessed from Main Menu, Help:

  • Online Help - Documentation: Opens the Documentation page.
  • Online Help - Search: Opens the Help search

Troubleshooting

If you are unable to access the manual by using the Help feature, try the following steps:

  • Restart your internet browser.
  • Restart your computer.
  • Clear the cookies/cache in your internet browser.
  • Switch your default internet browser.
