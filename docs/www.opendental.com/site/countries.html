International Customers

See Documentation.

Open Dental headquarters is located in the United States, but dentists around the world successfully use our software. Customers from countries outside of the United States can purchase the software directly from Open Dental. Please contact us via phone or email.

Fees and Support Options

See Fees for Support and Services. Fees and support options for countries outside the United States are reduced because of features, time zones, and/or language differences. All fees are in USD.

Some areas of the Caribbean are considered Other Countries for licensing reasons. (See Regional Licensing Issues.)
