Upload Postcard Recipients

Advertising Postcards allow an office to send marketing material to a specified list of existing or prospective patients.

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Tools, **Advertising - Postcards**.

![](images/postcardsUpload.png)

If Advertising - Postcards is already enabled, the window above will display. If Advertising - Postcards is not yet enabled, the [Postcards Setup (Advertising)](postcardssetup.html) window will open instead.

Also see

* [Send Postcards (Advertising)](sendpostcards.html)
* [Postcards (Advertising): Workflow](postcardsadvertisingworkflow.html)

**Setup**: Click to open the Advertising - Postcards Setup window.

Options:

* **Advertising - Postcards Account**: Click to select the Advertising - Postcards account to open.
* **Number of recipients**: The current number of patients selected to send postcards to.
  + **Select Patients**: Click to select which patients to send postcards to. See [Advertising - Select Patients](advertisingselectpatients.html).
* **List Name**: Give the patient list a name.

**View Account Details**: Click to send postcards without a pre-loaded patient list or view postcard account information.

**Upload List**: Click to send postcards using the list of selected patients.

Click **Cancel** to close without making changes.
