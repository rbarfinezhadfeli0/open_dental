EHR Vaccine Drug Manufacturers

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Setup, Chart, [EHR](ehrsetupwindow.html), then click **Drug Manufacturer**.

![](images/vaccineDrugManSetup.png)

Drug manufacturers must be set up before you can set up [Vaccines](ehrvaccine.html).

Click Add, or double click an existing manufacturer to edit.

![](images/vaccineDrugMan.png)

Enter the **Manufacturer Name** and **MVX Code**.

MVX Codes: <http://www2a.cdc.gov/vaccines/iis/iisstandards/vaccines.asp?rpt=mvx>
