Unearned Income Reports

In [Standard Reports](reportsstandard.html), in the *Monthly* area, click **Unearned Income**.

![](images/reportUnearned.png)

![](images/youtubeLogo.png) See our Webinar: [Reports: Unearned Income](https://youtu.be/hGlCnVSOSHE)

There are four types of [Unearned Income](unearnedprepayment.html) Reports.

* [Unearned Accounts Report](reportunearnedaccounts.html)
* [Unearned Allocation Report](reportunearnedallocation.html)
* [Net Unearned Income Report](reportnetunearnedincome.html)
* [Line Item Unearned Income Report](reportlineitemunearned.html)

Note: To determine user access to these reports, see [Report Setup: Security Permissions](reportsecurity.html). The Unearned Income reports use the [Complex Report System](reportcomplex.html).
