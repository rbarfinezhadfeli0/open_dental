Dental Student Reports

The Dental Schools Evaluation Report generates a list of student evaluation grades by dental course.

In [Evaluations](dsevaluations.html), click Reports.

![](images/dsReports.png)

See [Dental Schools](dentalschools.html).

Choose criteria to filter the report results:

* **Date Start** / **Date End**: Enter a date range to limit the report to.
* **Courses**: Highlight the [Dental School Courses](dscourses.html) to include in the report or check **All Courses** to include all. Affects the available Instructors.
* **Instructors**: Highlight the [Dental School Instructors](dsinstructors.html) to include in the report or check **All Instructors** to include all. Affects the available Students.
* **Students**: Highlight the [Dental School Students](dsstudents.html) to include in the report or click **All Students** to highlight all students in the list.

Click **OK** to generate the report.

Student evaluations matching the selected criteria are included in the report.

![](images/dsReportPrint.png)

* **Arrows**: Click the left/right arrow to jump pages in a multi-page report.
* **Query View**: Open the report in the [Query](queryoverview.html) window.
* **Export**: Save the report as a TXT or XLS file.
* **Print**: Print the report to the default printer.
