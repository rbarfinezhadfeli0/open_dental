Message-to-Pay Edit

Send messages via text or email to a patient with a direct link to the Payment Portal.

In the [Account Module](account.html), Payment dropdown, click **Send Message-to-Pay**.

![](images/msgToPayEdit.png)

When using the [Payment Portal](portalpaymentsbypatient.html), Message-to-Pay allows a patient to quickly make a payment without needing to log into the Patient Portal. See [Message-to-Pay: What Patient Sees](messagetopaysees.html) for more details.

Statement Type: Select what information to include on the statement.

* Family: Send statement information for all family members.
* Patient Only: Send statement information for the selected patient only.

Select one or both options to send a Message-to-Pay link. Text and email messages display beneath their respective boxes. Customize the default messages in [Payment Portal Setup](paymentportalsetup.html). Messages can be manually altered on a per-patient basis.

* **Send Email**: Send the patient an email message. A default email address must be enabled for the practice or clinic. Edit the email Subject as needed.
* **Send Text**: Send the patient a text message. Only available when integrated texting is enabled and the patient allows text messages.

**Edit Email Message**: Click to open the [HTML Email](emailhtml.html) Edit window.

**Preview Statement**: Click to preview the statement before sending.

**Send**: Click to send the message. A statement is immediately generated and can be viewed in the patient's account. The statement details use options found in [Billing Defaults](billingdefaults.html).
