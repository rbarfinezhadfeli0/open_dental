Family Health History

Use the Family Health History tab to document a patient's family problems.

In the [Medical](medical.html) window, select the Family History tab.

![](images/medicalFamilyHealthHistory.png)

Alternatively, in the [EHR Dashboard](ehrdashboard1.html) click Enter family history, and click the Family Health History tab.

This tab is only visible when EHR is enabled in [Show Features](showfeatures.html).

Click **Add Family History** to create a new entry or double-click an existing entry to edit. The Family Health Edit window is opened.

![](images/ehrFamilyHealthEdit.png)

Enter details for the Family Health problem:

* **Relationship:** Select the family member's relationship to the patient.
* **Name:** Enter the family member's name.
* **Problem:** Click **Pick** to select a problem from the [Problem List](problemmaster.html). Select *no problems known* to indicate health history has reviewed with a family member, even if there are no known problems.
* **SNOMED CT Code**: Read only. This field is automatically filled in when the attached problem is associated with a [SNOMED CT Code](snomedct.html).

Click **Save** to keep changes.
