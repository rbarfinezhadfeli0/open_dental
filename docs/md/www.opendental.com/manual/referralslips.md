Referral Slip

Create referral slips when referring patients to other providers.

In the [Referrals](referrals.html) grid, double-click. With a referral source selected, click **Referral Slip**.

![](images/referralSlip.gif)

Above is an example of the internal [Referral Slip Layout](sheetsreferralslips.html). Add notes in the pale yellow area as needed. Referral slips can be customized and associated to a specific referral source in the [Referral List](referralsetup.html).

Use the options at the bottom of the [Fill Sheet](sheetsfillout.html) window to send or print the referral slip.

The slip is saved in the [Account Module](account.html), [Communication Log](commlog.html), [Chart Module](chart.html), and in the [Edit Referral Attachment](referrals.html) window of the referral source.
