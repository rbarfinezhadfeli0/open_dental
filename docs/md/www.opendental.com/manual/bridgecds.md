Central Data Storage CDS Bridge

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Setup, [Program Links](programlinks.html). Double-click on **Central Data Storage**.

![](images/bridgeCDS.png)

Central Data Storage (CDS) is off-site STaaS (storage as a service). To sign up and begin using their services, visit their website: [Central Data Storage](https://www.centraldatastorage.com/open-dental/). Also see [Online Backups](backupsonline.html).

The only purpose of the CDS bridge is to remove the CDS button from the Backup window.

1. Verify that **Enabled** is NOT checked.
2. Check **Hide Unused Button**.
3. Click Save.
