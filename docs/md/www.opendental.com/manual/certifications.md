Certifications

Certifications are useful for tracking the progress of employee training.

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Tools, Certifications.

![](images/certifications.png)

**Setup**: Click to access [Certification Setup](certificationsetup.html).

**Show Only Incomplete**: Check to only view certifications for the selected employee with no date completed.

**Sort by Date Completed**: Check to sort certifications by date completed in descending order. Certifications with no date are listed below certifications with a date.

**Employee Search**: Search for an employee by first name to filter the Employee list. Leave blank to see all employees, not marked hidden, added to the [Employees List](employees.html).

**Report To**: Filter employees by who they report to (e.g., lead, supervisor, etc.)

**Employee**: Lists all [Employees](employees.html) matching the Employee Search and Reports To filters. Highlight an employee to view certification progress.

**Categories**: Select one or more categories to filter certifications by.

View By:

* **Categories**: View certifications by category. Displays the certification list as shown above.
* **Cert Complete**: View completed certifications by category and certification. Displays as shown below.

![](images/certificationsCertComplete.png)

**Print**: Print the certifications list. Must be printed in landscape to view all columns.

## Employee Certification

To mark a Certification as complete for an employee:

1. Ensure *View by Category* is selected.
2. Highlight the employee who completed the certification.
3. Double-click the certification from the grid on the right. The following window will open:
   ![](images/certificationsEmployee.png)
   * Enter the Date Completed or click **Today** to enter today's date.
   * Add any relevant notes about the certification.
   * Click **Save** to complete.

To delete a certification for an employee, double-click the certification from the grid, then click **Delete**.

If a certification was renewed (i.e., already had a date), double-click the certification and change the date.
