# ODMobile Feature

ODMobile (formerly Open Dental Mobile) is an [eService](eservices.html) that allows users to check their schedule, send prescriptions, and text patients, from their mobile device.

![](images/ODMobile.png)

## Download the App

Download on the [App Store for iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/open-dental/id1481881571?ls=1).
 Download on the [Play Store for Android.](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.opendental.opendental)

## Key Features

![](images/youtubeLogo.png) Watch our video: [A Quick Look at ODMobile](https://youtu.be/RhqU0_aHVe4).

* View and manage patient appointments
* View patient information, like contact details, upcoming appoinments, and more
* Two-way texting with patients directly from the app
* ePrescribe with [DoseSpot](dosespot.html) access
* View reports, like Production and Income and more
* And more!

Visit [ODMobile: What the Office Sees](0_odmobile.html) to take a closer look.

## Try out the App

Try out the app before signing up!

1. Download the ODMobile app to your mobile device.
2. Enter the username *Demo* when prompted for Signup Portal Login.
3. Enter the username *doctordemo* when prompted for Open Dental Login.
4. Enter any character as the password.

## Getting Started

ODMobile is included in our [eServices Bundle](eservicesbundle.html) or is available for a standalone monthly fee. See [Fees for Support and Services](fees.html#eservices) for pricing.

Practices must be on support and have a unique, active registration key to sign up for ODMobile and must remain on support while using the service.

ODMobile is a replacement for the [Mobile Web](mobileweb.html). If you were already using Mobile Web, you can switch to ODMobile at no additional cost.
