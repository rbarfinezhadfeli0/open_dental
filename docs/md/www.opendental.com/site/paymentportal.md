# Payment Portal Feature

The Payment Portal is an eService which allows patients to make payments online via a secure portal.

![](images/paymentPortalGraphic.png)

## Feature Highlights

With Payment Portal:

* Patients can easily access the portal from a dedicated link or from the Patient Portal.
* Your office can utilize the [Message-to-Pay Feature](messagetopay.html) to send patients an email or text message\* with their balance information and a link to make payments.
* Patients can view their account balance and quickly make payments online.

Visit [Payment Portal: What Patient Sees](0_paymentportalsees.html) to take a closer look.

*\*Requires the [Integrated Texting eService](textintegrated.html). Outgoing messages fees apply.*

## Getting Started

Payment Portal is available at no additional fee for practices on support with a unique, active registration key.

For a list of supported browsers see [Computer Requirements](computerrequirements.html).

Once you're ready, retrieve your Payment Portal URL from, then have your webmaster add the link to your office website.

Payment Portal is available when using an integrated [Credit Card Processor](nativepaymentintegrations.html).
