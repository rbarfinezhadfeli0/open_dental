# Electronic Dental Services (EDS)

Electronic Dental Services (EDS) is a clearinghouse available in Open Dental.

![](images/headerEDS.png)

EDS can be used within Open Dental for the following services:

* Electronic Claims: EDS can be used for dental and medical claims
* Electronic Claim Attachments
* Electronic Billing
* Electronic Remittance Advice (ERA)
* Electronic Eligibility and Benefits

## Getting Started

Contact EDS to sign up: [edsedi.com](http://www.edsedi.com/). EDS will supply the program and setup.
