# Web Sched New Patient: What Patient Sees

See [Web Sched Feature](websched.html).

## Scheduling an Appointment

Patients can click a link that navigates them to the Web Sched interface. This can be embedded on your own website or shared via text/email.

The patient is directed to answer prompts to determine what kind of appointment needs to be scheduled.
![](images/webSchedNewPatPrompts.png)

The patient can then search for available appointment times.
![](images/webschednewpatientCalendar.png)

Patients can enter their contact and insurance information. This information is synced back to Open Dental.
![](images/webSchedNewPatInfo.png)

Once the patient enters their information, their appointment is booked!
![](images/webschedNewPatientBooked.png)

After the appointment is booked, patients can be redirected to fill out intake forms via the [Web Forms Feature](webforms.html) and [Automated Messaging](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7automatedmessaging.html) can be used to send appointment reminders and confirmations.
