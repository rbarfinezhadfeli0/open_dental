# DentalXChange

DentalXChange is a [Clearinghouse](0_clearinghouses.html) integrated with Open Dental.

![](images/headerDentalXChange.png)

DentalXChange offers various electronic revenue cycle management solutions that are integrated with Open Dental. This includes:

* Claims
* Attachments
* Eligibility
* Electronic Remittance Advice (ERAs)
* Patient Statements
* Merchant Services

Visit [DentalXChange.com](https://www.dentalxchange.com/) for more information and to sign up.

## Claims

Through ClaimConnect by DentalXChange, you can easily validate and send electronic claims without leaving the Open Dental program.

## Attachments

Easily create and send electronic attachments for your insurance claims right from Open Dental. Even select images already saved to the Open Dental Imaging Module.

## Eligibility

Quickly process eligibility and benefits requests in Open Dental to ensure the accuracy of patient insurance benefits. Efficiently import benefits right to an insurance plan

## ERAs

Receive Electronic Remittance Advice directly in Open Dental from DentalXChange once you've enrolled with carriers. ERAs can even be automatically downloaded and processed to streamline workflow.

## Patient Statements

Send electronic statements right from the Open Dental Billing List to DentalXChange.

## Merchant Services

PayConnect by DentalXChange is integrated with Open Dental for easy processing of credit card payments. Payments are automatically posted to the Account Module.

## Getting Started

Contact [DentalXChange](https://www.dentalxchange.com/) to sign up for your preferred solutions. Once you're signed up, you can complete setup in Open Dental using credentials provided by DentalXChange.
