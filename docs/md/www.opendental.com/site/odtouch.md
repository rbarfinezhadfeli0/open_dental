# ODTouch Feature

ODTouch is an [eService](eservices.html) that uses a tablet-optimized app that serves as your clinical tool to complete tasks, like charting, scheduling, treatment planning, and more, from the convenience of a tablet.

![](images/odtouch.png)

ODTouch is available for US customers only.

![](images/youtubeLogo.png) Watch our videos: [A Quick Look at ODTouch](https://youtu.be/L6HfleM_S10) and [ODTouch Demo](https://youtu.be/xrCBZNZChCA).

## Download the App

Download on the [App Store for iOS](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/odtouch/id6444294687).
 Download on the [Play Store for Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.opendental.clinical).

## Key Features

* Complete routine appointment actions from a customizable checklist using the eRouting feature
* View and edit appointments
* Chart recommended treatment right from the app
* Generate and sign Payment Plans and Treatment Plans
* Create and view existing Perio Charts
* Review provider Production Goals and office Reports
* View documents and images stored in the [Imaging Module](0_images.html) from the app
* Enter patient Medical Information, like allergies and problems, from the app
* Use the device's microphone to easily dictate notes for any text field, such as procedure notes
* And more!

Visit [ODTouch: What the Office Sees](0_odtouch.html) to take a closer look.
