# Integrated Texting Feature

Open Dental's Integrated Texting [eService](eservices.html) allows you to send and receive text messages\* between patients and the office directly from Open Dental. It works in conjunction with other eServices for enhanced patient communication.

![](images/integratedTextingSiteHeader.png)

## Feature Highlights

![](images/youtubeLogo.png) Watch our video: [Integrated Texting Webinar](https://youtu.be/tk_1lSk1jIo) .

* Works with [Automated Messaging](ereminders.html) to send appointment reminders and other texts automatically\*\*
* Easily send appointment texts for all appointments for the day from the [Appointments Module](0_appointments.html)
* See texts from the Text Message Inbox and filter by patient
* [ODMobile](mobileapp.html) and [ODTouch](odtouch.html) users can text using the mobile apps\*\*
* [Web Sched](websched.html) users can send text messages with links\*\*

## Getting Started

A per-location access fee is included in [eServices Bundle](eservicesbundle.html) or is available for a standalone monthly fee. Additional fees apply per outbound text message (see [Integrated Texting Q and A](textmsgfaq.html)).

* United States, Canada, Puerto Rico see: [Fees for Software, Support, and Services](https://opendental.com/site/fees.html#eservices)
* Other Countries: See [International Fees](textintegratedfees.html).

Practices must be on support and have a unique, active registration key to sign up for Integrated Texting (see [eServices Signup](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7eservicessignup.html)).

![](images/texting.png)

\*Text messages are not a secure method of sending PHI. See [Encryption of Data at Rest and in Transit](encryption.html).

\*\*Additional eService required.
