Medical

The medical area is where staff can imput important patient medical information like current medication, problems, allergies, etc.

In the [Chart Module](0_chart.html), double-click the pink Medical area. Click the Medical Info tab.

![](images/medical.png)

## Medical Info Tab

Use the Medical Info tab (shown above) to record general medical information about the patient.

* **Premedicate**: Easily indicate if a patient needs to be premedicated. This flag can also be displayed on appointments for quick reference.
* **Med Urgent**: Urgent medical notes about the patient. This information can be displayed on appointments for quick reference.

## Problems Tab

From the Problems tab, you can track any medical conditions or problems a patient may have.

![](images/medicalProblems.png)

Problems are selected from a customizable master list.

## Medications Tab

From the Medications tab, add medications the patient is taking.

![](images/medicalMedications.png)

Medications are automatically added when creating a prescription in Open Dental or when using [eRx](electronicrx.html). Other medications (e.g., over-the-counter or prescriptions from other providers) can be added to the patient from a customizable master list.

## Allergies Tab

Track patient allergies from the Allergies tab.

![](images/medicalAllergies.png)

Allergies are selected from a customizable master list.

## Vital Signs

Track patient vital signs (pulse, blood pressure, etc.) that are recorded during a visit.

![](images/medicalVitalSigns.png)

## Medical Information in the Chart

Medical information is displayed in the Patient Info area of the [Chart Module](0_chart.html).

![](images/medicalPanel.png)

The displayed fields and background color of the medical area are customizable.
