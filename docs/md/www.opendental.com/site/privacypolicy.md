Privacy Policy - Google

Also see: [Computer Security](securitycomputers.html)

Last updated 01/02/2020

When you provide Google account information for accessing email, the data we access using your Google account is limited to sending and displaying that email to you. We do not collect or retain any of the information used. The purpose of using your Google account information, at your request, is to improve your experience by allowing you to partially integrate your email account into Open Dental.

App's use of information received from Gmail APIs will adhere to [Google's Limited Use Requirements](https://developers.google.com/terms/api-services-user-data-policy#additional-requirements-for-specific-api-scopes).
