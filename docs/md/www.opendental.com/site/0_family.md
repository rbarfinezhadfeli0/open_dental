Family Module

The Family [Module](0_modules.html) is where basic patient and insurance information is entered and organized.

![](images/family.png)

![](images/youtubeLogo.png) See our [Family Module Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLyjZ9PCVynCmRWyNcN1QW9Rttvl6cpBOd).

## Family Toolbar

![](images/familyToolbar.png)

**Family Members**: Add and delete patients, set a family member as the guarantor, and move a patient to a different family.

**Clones**: Create and manage [Patient Clones](0_clone.html).

**Super Family**: Create and manage a [Super Family](0_superfamily.html). This feature is used to group multiple families into an overarching group. This is helpful for billing.

**Add Insurance**: Attach an insurance plan or view all insurance plans for the family.

**Discount Plan**: Attach a [Discount Plan](0_discountplan.html) to the patient. This feature can be used to automatically apply treatment discounts for patients without insurance.

## Patient Picture & Family Member List

![](images/familyPatientPic.png)

View the patient's photo and quickly switch between family members.

## Recall

![](images/familyRecall.png)

View the patient's [Recall](0_recall.html) information, including due dates and scheduled dates.

## Patient Information

A summary of patient information, including contact information. Open the [Patient Information](0_patientedit.html) by double-clicking the area to edit information. Available fields in this area are customizable.

![](images/familyPatInfo.png)

This area also lists referrals and [CareCredit](carecredit.html) information.

## Insurance Plans

The Insurance Plans area displays all insurance plans currently attached to the patient as active coverage. A nearly unlimited amount of plans can be attached (primary, secondary, tertiary, primary medical, secondary medical, etc.) Below the plan details is the insurance history area.

![](images/familyInsArea.png)

## Discount Plans

This area replaces the Insurance Plans area when a [Discount Plan](0_discountplan.html) has been attached to the patient.

![](images/familyDiscPlan.png)
