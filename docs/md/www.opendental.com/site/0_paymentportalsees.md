# Payment Portal: What Patient Sees

The Payment Portal allows patients to make online payments with an integrated credit card processor.

In a web browser, launch the Payment Portal.

![](images/paymentPortalPatientSelect.png)

The Payment Portal uses a unique link specific to the practice. The URL can be embedded on the practice's website, sent via email, or accessed via Patient Portal.

## Pay Now or Verify and Pay

After entering patient details, patients can make a one-time payment without viewing their balance or can verify their information to view their balance and save their payment method for future use.

![](images/paymentPortalPayOption.png)

## Pay Now

If Pay Now is selected, the patient is taken to the payment amount screen.

![](images/paymentPortalHowMuch.png)

From here, patients can enter their card number and continue with payment. Screens and fields may vary depending on the payment processor.

![](images/paymentPortalCardInfo.png)

## Verify and Pay

If Verify and Pay is selected, the patient is sent a verification code to the patient information on file.

![](images/paymentPortalVerificationCode.png)

They can then choose to pay their balance, or a specific amount.

![](images/paymentPortalAmount.png)

Next, the patient can select from an existing card, add a new card, or delete cards no longer in use. For PaySimple users, a bank account can be used as well.

![](images/paymentPortalChoose.png)

## Receipt

The result of the payment (e.g., successful, unsuccessful) will display. The patient will receive an emailed receipt, or they can print a receipt from this window.

![](images/paymentPortalReceipt.png)
