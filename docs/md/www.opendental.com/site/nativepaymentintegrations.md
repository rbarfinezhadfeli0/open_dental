# Native Payment Integrations

Open Dental is integrated with three merchant service companies to process payments directly through Open Dental.

![](images/headerPaymentIntegrations.png)

Open Dental works with vendors to develop and support Native Payment Integrations.

With a Native Payment Integration:

* Payments can be processed directly through Open Dental
* Payment details (e.g., credit card numbers or bank account information) can be saved to Open Dental to process future payments or recurring charges.
* Payments are automically posted to the patient account in Open Dental

## Integrated Options

Open Dental has native integrations with three payment processors. Prior to using an integrated payment method, your practice must first sign up with the merchant and enable the program link.

More than one credit card processing program may be enabled in Open Dental and clinics may set up different credentials when using different merchant accounts.

### Global Payments

Formerly EdgeExpress (OpenEdge)

* Available internationally, including Canada.
* Supports EMV transactions.
* Supports online patient payments through the [Payment Portal](paymentportal.html).
* Supports Recurring Charges.

See [Global Payments](globalpaymentsintegration.html) for more information.
 Website: [globalpaymentsintegrated.com](https://www.globalpaymentsintegrated.com/)

### PayConnect by DentalXChange

* Available only in the United States.
* Supports EMV transactions.
* Supports online patient payments through the [Payment Portal](paymentportal.html).
* Supports Recurring Charges.

See [PayConnect by DentalXChange](dentalxchange.html#payconnect) for more information.
 Website: [dentalxchange.com](http://www.dentalxchange.com/)

### PaySimple

[PaySimple](paysimpleintegration.html): Available only in the United States.

* Supports swiped credit card transactions plus ACH and direct debit payments from a patient's checking or savings account.
* Supports online patient payments through the [Payment Portal](paymentportal.html).
* Supports Recurring Charges.

See [PaySimple](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7paysimple.html) for more information.
 Website: [paysimple.com](https://paysimple.com/partner/open-dental)

### Additional Information

Other merchant services may utilize Open Dental's API to develop their own integration. Open Dental is not directly involved in the development or support of these integrations. See [Third-Party Vendors](vendorsthirdparty.html) for more details.
