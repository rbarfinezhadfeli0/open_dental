Version 23.1

See [Versions](versions.html).

Version 23.1 was released on 07/31/2023 .

## Major Changes

**Enhanced Online Payments**

* New Payment Portal. [Payment Portal](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7portalpaymentsbypatient.html)
* Compatible with XCharge, EdgeExpress, PayConnect, and PaySimple. [Payment Portal Setup](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7paymentportalsetup.html)
* Accessible via direct link or via Patient Portal. [Patient Portal: What Patient Sees](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7portalpatientsees.html)

**PayConnect 2.0 Integration**

* Added integration with PayConnect 2.0. [PayConnect Setup](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7payconnectsetup.html)
* Updated process when taking payments. [PayConnect Window](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7payconnectpaymentwindow.html)
* Updated process when voiding and returning payments. [PayConnect Void](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7payconnectvoid.html), [Credit Card Return](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7paymentreturn.html)

**New ODTouch eService App**

* Currently only available for Limited Beta customers. [ODTouch](https://opendental.com/site/odtouch.html)
* Seamlessly take patients through their appointment by performing routine appointment actions. [ODTouch: Home Screen](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7odtouchhome.html)
