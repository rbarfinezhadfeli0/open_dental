# Submit a Payment

When you order the full version of Open Dental, you will eventually submit payment for [Software Support and Services Fees](fees.html). There are several options for paying your Open Dental support statement.

## Payment Methods

You can submit payment to us over the phone, by fax, mail, or email\* (credit/debit).

## Accepted Payment Types

**Debit card:** Pay using your debit card by phone, fax, mail, or email\*.
**Credit card:** Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. Pay using a credit card by phone, fax, mail, or email\*.

Note: Call [Support](contact.html) if you would like to set up automated monthly payments on a debit or credit card.

**Check:** Send a check in the mail to pay your bill (U.S. only). Make checks payable to Open Dental.

\*Email is not a secure method of sending credit/debit card information. We prefer to not receive card information via email.
