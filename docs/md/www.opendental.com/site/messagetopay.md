# Message-to-Pay Feature

Send patients a text message or email to quickly make their payment via the Payment Portal.

![](images/MessagetoPayGraphic.png)

Message-to-Pay is available in Version 24.1 and greater.

## Feature Highlights

Message-to-Pay allows patients to do the following:

* Easily make a payment securely from their mobile device or computer
* Opt to make a one-time payment or save a card on file for future payments
* View their balance or statement

Visit [Message-to-Pay: What Patient Sees](0_messagetopaysees.html) to take a closer look.

## Getting Started

Message-to-Pay is available via email at no additional fee for practices on support with a unique, active registration key. For text messages, Integrated Texting fees apply. See [Fees for Software, Support, and Services](fees.html#eservices).

Start by enabling the [Payment Portal](paymentportal.html). Message-to-Pay and the Payment Portal are available when using a [Native Payment Integration](nativepaymentintegrations.html).

For a list of supported browsers see [Computer Requirements](computerrequirements.html).
