# Web Sched ASAP: What Patient Sees

See [Web Sched Feature](websched.html).

1. Patient receives email or text messages about an available opening:
   ![](images/webSchedASAPEmail.png)
2. Patient clicks link to open Web Sched ASAP.
   ![](images/webschedASAPUI.gif)
3. Patient has the option to accept or decline the appointment(s). They can also opt out of future ASAP notifications for this appointment. The patient will be unable to schedule if the available appointment has already been taken by another patient.

### In Open Dental

The rescheduled appointment instantly shows in the appointment schedule and the appointment is no longer marked ASAP.
