# ODTouch: What the Office Sees

Use ODTouch to access appointment actions for a patient including treatment plans, charting, and more.

In ODTouch, the Home Screen opens upon login.

![](images/odtouchHome.png)

In the Home Screen, you can interact with daily appointments, select patients, view provider goals, and go through appointment actions for the selected patient.

## Treatment Plans

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Tx Plans.

![](images/odtouchTxPlans.png)

You can create, edit, and save [Treatment Plans](0_treatmentplan.html) to print or have the patient sign in the app.

## Perio Charts

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Perio.

![](images/odtouchPerio.png)

Using this screen, you can create [Perio Exams](0_periochart.html), use voice perio charting, and view summary and graphical perio charts.

## Payment Plans

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Pay Plans.

![](images/odtouchPayPlan.png)

Create or modify [Payment Plans](0_paymentplan.html) from scratch or by using a template, then print the payment plan or have the patient sign it in the app.

## Images

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Images.

![](images/odTouchImages.png)

View images from the [Imaging Module](0_images.html), capture new images, or edit existing image information.

## Exam Sheets

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Exam Sheet.

![](images/odtouchExam.png)

You can create custom [Exam Sheets](0_examsheet.html) that can also be filled out in the app.

## eRouting

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap eRouting.

![](images/odtoucheRouting.png)

Using eRouting, you can create custom checklists of appointment actions to take for patients.

## Medical Information

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Medical.

![](images/odtouchMedicalInfo.png)

From this screen, you can add [Medical Information](0_medical.html) for your patient such as any notes, problems, medications, or allergies the patient has. Vital signs can also be tracked here.

## eRx

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap eRx.

![](images/odtoucheRx.png)

You can use the [DoseSpot eRx Feature](dosespot.html) to create and send prescriptions directly from the app.

## Progress Notes and Charting Treatment

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Chart.

![](images/odtouchChart.png)

From this screen, you can interact with the progress notes from the [Chart Module](0_chart.html) as well as create prescriptions to print and consent forms for the patient to sign.

### Charting Procedures

In ODTouch: Chart, in the bottom right, tap the edit icon.

![](images/odtouchChartEdit.png)

Using the Chart Edit screen, you can mark teeth as missing/hidden, change the dentition, or chart procedures by using procedure buttons or entering the procedure codes.

## Appointments

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Appts.

![](images/odtouchAppts.png)

Easily create and [Edit Appointments](0_apptedit.html) found in the [Appointments Module](0_appointments.html), including adding procedures, [Lab Cases](0_labcases.html), and other details.

## Reports

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Reports.

![](images/odtouchReports.png)

You can run basic reports to get a quick look at production, income, and more.

## Integrated Texting

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Messages.

![](images/odtouchMessages.png)

Using the [Integrated Texting Feature](textintegrated.html), send and receive text messages from patients in the app.
