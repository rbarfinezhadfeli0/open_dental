# EHR Objective 10: Public Health Reporting

This information applies to EHR Modified Stage 2 for Program Years 2015 to 2018. For current information, see:

* [EHR Modified Stage 2](ehrmodified2.html)
* [2019 Program Requirements - Medicaid](https://www.cms.gov/Regulations-and-Guidance/Legislation/EHRIncentivePrograms/2019ProgramRequirementsMedicaid.html)
* [Open Dental EHR](https://opendental.com/site/ehrlicense.html)

The EP is in active engagement with a public health agency to submit electronic public health data from the CEHRT, except where prohibited and in accordance with applicable law and practice.

**Measure Option 1 - Immunization Registry Reporting** Provider is in active engagement with a public health agency to submit immunization data.

**Exclusion?**

Yes, if provider doesn't administer vaccinations or operates in a jurisdiction that doesn't accept immunization data from EP.

**Measure Option 2 - Syndromic Surveillance Reporting** Provider is in active engagement with a public health agency to submit syndromic surveillance data.

Exclusion? Yes, if provider is in a category of providers from which syndromic surveillance data is not collected or operates in a jurisdiction in which no public health agency is capable of or ready to receive syndromic surveillance data.

**Measure Option 3 - Specialized Registry Reporting** Provider is in active engagement to submit data to a specialized registry.

**Exclusion?**

Yes, if provider does not diagnose or treat any disease or condition associated with a specialized registry, or operates in a jurisdiction where no specialized registry is capable of or ready to receive electronic registry data.

Note: It is possible that dentists will meet exclusion criteria for all public health measures. Even if you intend to claim an exclusion, you may need to sign participation agreements or addendum's. For a full list of public health agencies by state, see <http://www.aphl.org/programs/informatics/Pages/MU-Public-Health-Readiness.aspx>.

Contact your state early to determine requirements, sign legal agreements, or begin the onboarding process.
