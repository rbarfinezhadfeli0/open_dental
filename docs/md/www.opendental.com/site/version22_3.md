Version 22.3

See [Versions](versions.html).

Version 22.3 was released on 12/20/2022.

![](images/youtubeLogo.png) Overview of [Version 22.3](https://youtu.be/OaqOQJwCXnc).

## Major Changes

**Quickly Receive Claims and Preauthorizations as No Payment and Zero Out Write-offs.**

* Use the Outstanding Insurance Report to clean up no payment claims. [Outstanding Insurance Claims Report](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7reportoutins.html)
* Adjustment Picker filters out adjustments attached to Dynamic Payment Plans. [Procedure - Financial Tab](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7procedurefinancial.html)

**Automatically Zero Out Write-off Estimates when annual max, age limitation, or frequency limitation is met.**

* Preference to zero out write-offs for insurance estimates when annual max is met. [Preferences](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7preferences.html), [Insurance Plan](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7insplan.html)
* Preference to zero out write-offs when age or frequency limitation is met. [Preferences](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7preferences.html), [Insurance Plan](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7insplan.html)

**Added 2023 CDT Codes**. (Also backported to 22.2.43) [Procedure Code Tools](../autoLogin.aspx%EF%B9%96ReturnUrl%3D%EA%A4%B7help253%EA%A4%B7procedurecodetools.html)

* ![](images/youtubeLogo.png) QuickTip: [How to update CDT Codes](https://youtu.be/6BkLSTcTyjE).
