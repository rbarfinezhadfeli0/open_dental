# Patient Portal: What Patient Sees

The Patient Portal allows patients to view their appointments, treatment plans, statements, and more.

In an internet browser, navigate to the Patient Facing URL.

![](images/portalLogIn.gif)

Patients can access the Patient Portal using most web browsers or mobile devices. On the login screen, patients can view office name and contact information, click a link to the [Payment Portal](0_paymentportalsees.html), and log in to the Patient Portal. Below is a summary of what the patient or authorized representatives may see when they go to the Patient Portal.

## Navigating Patient Portal

Patients can navigate Patient Portal by clicking the tabs at the top. Tabs can be included or omitted depending on the settings selected by the office. Patients can also view contact information for the practice or clinic, change their password, or view patient information for other family members.

![](images/patientPortalMain.gif)

## Appointments

In the Appointments tab, patients can view scheduled and completed appointments and view other action needed items, such as appointments that need confirmed and [Web Sched Recall](websched.html#recall) notifications that are still unscheduled.

![](images/patPortalAppointments.png)

## Account

In the Account area, patients can view statements and make payments using the [Payment Portal](0_paymentportalsees.html).

![](images/portalAccount.png)

## Plan

In the Plan tab, patients can view treatment planned procedures saved from the [Treatment Plan Module](0_treatmentplan.html).

![](images/portalTP.gif)

## WebMail

In the WebMail tab, patients can view and send private [WebMail](securewebmail.html) or [Secure Email](secureemail.html) messages (between the provider and patient).

![](images/portalWebMail.gif)

## Summary

In the Summary tab, patients can view, download, or transmit Continuity of Care Document (CCD).

![](images/portalCareSummary.gif)

## Images

In the Images tab, patients can view PDFs and image files stored in the [Imaging Module](0_images.html) folders shared to the Patient Portal.

![](images/portalImages.gif)
