# Web Sched Feature

Web Sched [eServices](eservices.html), powered by PatientViewer, allow patients to schedule appointments online. Each service has additional per month/per location fees.

![](images/webSchedHeader.png)

## Getting Started

There are four separate Web Sched eServices, each available for an individual monthly fee or as part of the [eService bundle](eservicesbundle.html). See [Fees for Support and Services](fees.html#eservices) for pricing.

Practices must be on support and have a unique, active registration key to sign up for Web Sched eServices. Practices must remain on support while using the service.

## Web Sched Recall

![](images/youtubeLogo.png) Watch our video: [A Quick Look at Web Sched Recall](https://youtu.be/yZvlozVRdN8) .

* Manage recall reminders\* and appointment scheduling by allowing patients to schedule their recall appointments online.
* Reminders with a Web Sched Recall link can be sent manually from the [Recall List](0_recalllist.html) or set up to be sent automatically.
* Includes access to [Web Sched ASAP](#asap) feature at no additional cost.
* Scheduled appointments are added to Open Dental automatically.
* Receive alerts in Open Dental when appointments are scheduled.

Visit [Web Sched Recall: What Patient Sees](0_webschedrecallsees.html) to take a closer look.

\*Text recall reminders require [Integrated Texting](textintegrated.html).

## Web Sched New Patient

* Allow new patients to schedule their first appointment online via your website.
* Once a patient enters their information online, a new patient record is created in Open Dental.
* Scheduled appointments are added to Open Dental automatically.
* Send an optional notification to patients with appointment details automatically.
* Receive alerts in Open Dental when appointments are scheduled.

Visit [Web Sched New Patient: What Patient Sees](0_webschednewpatsees.html) to take a closer look.

## Web Sched Existing Patient

* Allow existing patients to schedule their appointments online via your website.
* Scheduled appointments are added to Open Dental automatically.
* Send an optional notification to patients with appointment details automatically.
* Receive alerts in Open Dental when appointments are scheduled.

Visit [Web Sched Existing Patient: What Patient Sees](0_webschedexistingpatientsees.html) to take a closer look.

## Web Sched ASAP

![](images/youtubeLogo.png) Watch our video: [A Quick Look at Web Sched ASAP](https://youtu.be/9tSUCVbEn4k).

* Easily send emails or texts\* from the [ASAP List](0_asaplist.html) to notify patients of an appointment opening.
* Patients receive a unique link to claim or decline the opening.
* A blockout is created on the schedule when Web Sched ASAP notifications are sent, and you can easily check Web Sched ASAP History to find messages sent and appointment slots filled.
* Receive alerts in Open Dental when appointments are scheduled.
* Web Sched ASAP messages can be customized for your clinic's needs.

Visit [Web Sched ASAP: What Patient Sees](0_webschedasappatientsees.html) to take a closer look.

\*Text messages require [Integrated Texting](textintegrated.html).

Note: Can be purchased stand-alone for a fee, or is included free with [Web Sched Recall](#recall).

Want to try out Web Sched before setting it up in your database? Check out our demos before signing up for the eServices and setting it up in your database.

* [WebSched Recall Demo](http://www.patientviewer.com/wsrdemo.html)
* [Web Sched New Patient Demo](http://www.patientviewer.com/wsnpdemo.html)
* [WebSched ASAP Demo](http://www.patientviewer.com/wsasapdemo.html)

For Web Sched Existing Patient and Web Sched New Patient, PatientViewer can also be embedded in a website to enhance its appearance. Check out this [Example Website](http://patientviewer.com/demo/wsnp_embedded). Your IT and/or webmaster can help you set this up. Open Dental is not able to embed or manage custom websites for your practice.
