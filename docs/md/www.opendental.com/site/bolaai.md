# Bola AI

Bola Voice Assist is integrated into the Open Dental [Perio Chart](0_periochart.html).

![](images/headerBolaAI.png)

Bola Voice Assist allows for seamless voice-command functionality, making perio charting more efficient and hands-free.

## Getting Started

First, contact Bola AI to sign up: [bola.ai](https://www.opendental.com/site/bola.ai)

Once signed up, enable the Bola AI bridge in Open Dental.

Launch Bola AI Voice Assist from the Perio Chart in Open Dental. Perio measurements are saved to Open Dental.
