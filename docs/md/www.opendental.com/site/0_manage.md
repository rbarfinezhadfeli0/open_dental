Manage Module

The Manage [Module](0_modules.html) contains miscellaneous management functions that do not apply to any single patient.

![](images/manage.png)

![](images/YouTube_PlayButton_15px.png) See our [Manage Module Playlist](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLyjZ9PCVynCn2gpkJ9PyahkXpbjHj9AK5)

## Daily Area

Find tools to help manage the day-to-day operations of the practice in the Daily area.

![](images/manageDaily.png)

* **Send Claims:** View a list of claims waiting to be sent and quickly batch send or print. This window also shows history of sent or printed claims.
* **Batch Ins:** Enter Batch [Insurance Payments](0_insurancepayments.html).
* **Billing:** Print or email batch statements and set billing defaults. See [Billing](0_billing.html).
* **Deposits:** Create and manage deposit slips. Deposit slips can be printed with bank account information.
* **Supply Inventory:** Open [Supply Inventory](0_supplyinventory.html) to track suppliers, supplies, orders, and equipment.
* **TSI Collections:** Launch the TSI Collections integration. Transworld Systems Inc. (TSI) offers billing and collection services to dental practices.
* **Tasks:** Opens the [Tasks](0_tasks.html). Tasks can be used for intra-office communication, settings reminders, and more.
* **Backup:** Create a backup of your Open Dental database using the built-in [Backup Tool](0_backup.html).
* **Accounting:** Open the [Accounting](0_accounting.html) feature used to set up a chart of accounts and track expenses, income, assets, liabilities and equity.
* **Email Inbox:** Open the [Email Client](0_email.html). You can set up email accounts that your practice uses to send and receive emails using Open Dental.
* **ERAs:** Open the [ERAs](0_era.html) window to process and manage ERAs.

## Time Clock

In the Manage Module, at the upper-right, is the Time Clock area.

![](images/manageTimeClock.png)

Employees can use the [Time Clock](0_timecards.html) to clock in or out of work.

## Messaging

In the Manage Module, at the bottom, is the Messaging area.

![](images/manageMessaging.png)

Use [Messaging Buttons](0_messagingbuttons.html) to send internal text, light, and sound messages to any workstation in the office.
