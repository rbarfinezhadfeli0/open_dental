Definitions: Supply Categories

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Setup, [Definitions](definitions.html), Supply Categories.

![](images/definitionsSupplyCats.png)

The Supply Categories definition determines the category options available to group [Supplies](supplies.html).

Double-click an existing item or click **Add** to create a new item. The Edit Definition window will open.

![](images/definitionEditSupplyCats.png)

Enter the category **Name** then click OK to save.

Click **Delete** to remove a category not in use.
