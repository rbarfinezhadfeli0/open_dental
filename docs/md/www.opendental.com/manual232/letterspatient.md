Letter

In the [Main Toolbar](toolbar.html), click Letter.

![](images/lettersPick.gif)

Before creating or sending a patient letter, set up at least one custom Patient Letter sheet. See [Patient Letter Layout](sheetsletterspatient.html).

A list of custom sheets that have a type of *PatientLetter* will show. Double-click a letter to select and open it to fill out.

* The default content of the letter is defined in the sheet setup.
* Text on the letter is editable. Click in the letter and begin to type to make changes.

![](images/LetterFill.gif)

Use the options at the bottom of the [Fill Sheet](sheetsfillout.html) window to send the letter. Sent letters can be viewed in the patient's Chart Module and Account Module.
