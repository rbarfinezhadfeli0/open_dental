Definitions: Claim Payment Tracking

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Setup, [Definitions](definitions.html), Claim Payment Tracking.

![](images/definitionsClaimPaymentTracking.png)

The Claim Payment Tracking definition determines which payment tracking options are available for [Claim Procedures ( claimprocs )](claimprocedure.html). The *Default* type is added by default. Add additional Claim Payment Tracking types as needed.

Double-click an existing item or click **Add** to create a new item. The Edit Definition window will open.

![](images/definitionEditClaimPayTrack.png)

Enter the tracking **Name** then click OK to save.

Unused items can be marked as **Hidden**. At least one item must be left unhidden.
