Edit Patient Information: EHR Misc Tab

The EHR Misc tab is used for immunization data.

In the [Edit Patient Information](patientedit.html) window, click the EHR Misc tab.

![](images/patientEditEhrMisc.gif)

This tab is only visible when EHR is turned on in [Show Features](showfeatures.html).

**Mother's Maiden First Name**: The patient's mother's first name.

**Mother's Maiden Last Name**: The patient's mother's maiden last name.

**Date Time Deceased**: Entering a date will lock the patient's age as of the deceased date. This information is also used for Syndromic Surveillance data.
