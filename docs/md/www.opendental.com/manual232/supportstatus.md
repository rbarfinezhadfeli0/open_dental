Support Status

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Help, Support Status.

![](images/supportStatus.png)

The support status window displays the current Open Dental registration key and whether the office has an active [Paid Support](fees.html) account.

Note: An active internet connection is required to view support status.
