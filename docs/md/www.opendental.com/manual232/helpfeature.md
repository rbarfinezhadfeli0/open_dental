Help Feature

The Help Feature allows easy access to the manual and FAQs. It is available for customers on Support.

In [Open Dental](mainmenu.html), at the top of a window, click the **?** icon.

![](images/helpWindow.png)

When clicked, the corresponding manual page opens.

If a manual page has FAQs included, they appear at the bottom of the page.

![](images/helpFAQ.png)

Click a question to expand it.
