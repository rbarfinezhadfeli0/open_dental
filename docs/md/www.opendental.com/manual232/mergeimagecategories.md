Merge Image Categories

Image categories can be merged together to combine images into one folder.

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Tools, Misc Tools, Merge Image Categories.

![](images/mergeImageCats.png)

Merging image categories logs an entry in the [Audit Trail](audittrail.html).

To merge Image Categories:

1. **Image Category to merge into**: Click **Change** to select the Image Category to keep.
2. **Image Category to merge from**: Click **Change** to select the Image Category to remove.
3. Click **Merge** to combine the categories. A warning prompts you to confirm.

![](images/mergeImageCatsConfirm.png)4. Click **Yes** to continue.

Once the merge is complete, all images from the *Image Category to merge from* (Step 2) are now moved to the *Image Category to merge into* (Step 1). The *Image Category to merge from* is also marked as Hidden in [Definitions: Image Categories](definitionsimagecat.html).
