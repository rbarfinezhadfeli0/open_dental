Dental School Classes

Dental School Classes refer to the group of students (e.g., Hygiene Class of 2023).

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Lists, Dental School Classes.

![](images/dentalClasses.png)

[Dental Schools](dentalschools.html) must be enabled in [Show Features](showfeatures.html) for this window to display.

Click **Add** or double-click a class to edit.

![](images/dentalClassAdd.png)

* **Graduation Year**: Enter the class graduation year.
* **Description**: Enter a description (e.g., Dental or Hygiene).

Click **OK** to save.

**Delete**: Click to remove the class. Classes can only be deleted if no students have been assigned the class in [Dental School Providers](dsproviders.html).
