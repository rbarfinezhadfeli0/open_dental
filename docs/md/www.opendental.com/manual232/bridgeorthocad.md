OrthoCAD Bridge

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Setup, [Program Links](programlinks.html). Double-click on **OrthoCAD from www.itero.com**.

![](images/bridgeOrthoCad.png)

OrthoCAD is digital imaging technology for orthodontics. Website: <http://www.itero.com/en-us>.

Note: OrthoCAD required an additional program, called CaseManager, to function as a bridge. CaseManager was discontinued in April 2023. As a result, the OrthoCAD bridge is no longer functional.
