eServices Sign Up About

The *About* tab displays read-only information based on your registration key.

In the [Sign Up Portal](eservicessignup.html), select the **About** tab.

![](images/signupportalAbout.gif)

This is used by Open Dental technical support for troubleshooting [eServices](../site/eservices.html) issues.

**Program Version**: The version of Open Dental.

**Last eConnector Heartbeat**: The last time the eConnector heartbeat was detected.

**Legacy Type**: Historical billing information.

**Listener Type**: Indicates what the eConnector is doing.

**Permission Level**: The permission level of the logged on user. Those with Full Access (*Security Admin* permission) can change eServices. Those without have read-only access.

**Is Reseller Customer**?: Indicates if there is a reseller registration key.

**On Support**?: Indicates whether the location is currently on Open Dental support.
