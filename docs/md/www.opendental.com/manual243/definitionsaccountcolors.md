Definitions: Account Colors

Customize text colors of payments, claims, and more in the Patient Account grid using Account Colors.

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Setup, [Definitions](definitions.html), Account Colors.

![](images/definitionsAccountColors.png)

The Account Colors definition determines the text color of line items in the [Account Module](account.html), Patient Account grid.

Definitions:

* **Default:** Line items that are not listed below.
* **Adjustments**: Text color of [Adjustment](adjustments.html).
* **Discounts**: Text color of [TP Procedure Discount](treatmentplandiscounts.html) adjustments.
* **Patient Payments**: Text color of a Patient [Payment](payment.html).
* **Insurance**: [Claim](claimedit.html) with a status of *Waiting to Send* or *Sent*.
* **Comm**: Text color of [Statement](statement.html).
* **PayPlan**: Payment plan *Charges Due* and *Production*
* **Insurance Payment**: Text color of [Finalize Insurance Payment](claimpayfinalize.html).
* **Received Ins Claim**: Claims with a status of *received*.
* **Received Pre-Auth**: [Preauthorization](preauth.html) with a status of *received*. Preauthorizations no longer show in the Account Module, this definition is permanently hidden.
* **Broken Appointment Procedure**: Procedure D9986 (missed appointment).
* **Canceled Appointment Procedure**: Procedure D9987 (canceled appointment).

Double-click an item to edit the color.

![](images/definitionAccountColorEdit.png)

Click the colored block to select a [Color](colorselection.html). Click **Save** to exit the window and keep any changes. The item cannot be renamed or hidden.
