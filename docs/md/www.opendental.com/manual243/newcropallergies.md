NewCrop eRx Allergies

Ensora eRx (formerly NewCrop eRx) users can add allergies to a patient's profile in order to get drug-allergy interaction alerts when prescribing drugs.

In the [Ensora eRX / Prescription](newcrop.html) interface, under Pending Rx, is an Allergies area.

![](images/erxAllergyList.png)

Ensora eRx uses the allergies entered here when checking for [Drug-Allergy Interaction Checks](newcropdruginteraction.html). Allergies entered in Open Dental are not passed to Ensora eRx. Allergies must be added to Ensora eRx manually.

To add an allergy:

1. Click **Add** in the Allergies section of the Compose Rx tab.
2. Enter all or part of the allergy (brand, ingredient, or drug category such as NSAID) and click  **Allergy Search**. Alternatively, click **Common Allergies** to choose from a list.
![](images/erxAllergyEnter.png)3. Select the allergy from the search results and enter details about the patient's allergy.
![](images/eRxAllergyDetail.png)4. Click **Save Allergy** to add it to the list.

Alternatively, allergies can be viewed and added from the Problems tab in the Ensora eRx window.
