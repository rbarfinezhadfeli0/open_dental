Post-n-Track E-Claims

Post-n-Track, also known as PNT Data, is an E-Claims clearinghouse.

In [Clearinghouses](clearinghouses.html), double-click Post-n-Track.

![](images/eclaimsPost-n-Track.png)

Visit their website: [www.pntdata.com](http://www.pntdata.com/).
