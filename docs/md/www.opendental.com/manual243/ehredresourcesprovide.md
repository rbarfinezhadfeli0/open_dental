EHR Educational Resources

On the [EHR Dashboard](ehrdashboard1.html), click **Provide education resources**.

![](images/ehrEdResources.gif)

To provide an education resource, the patient must meet criteria that triggers a resource.

* See [Setting up Education Resources and Triggers](ehrsetupedresources.html).
* For EHR measure details, see [EHR Objective 6: Patient-Specific Education](../site/ehredresources.html).

All education resources triggered based on this patient's problems, medications, lab results, or tobacco status are listed at the top.

Click on a link to open a resource in a browser window.

Click **Print**, then provide the resource to the patient. A dated log entry will list under Education Provided and the numerator in Measure Reports for Education will increase.
