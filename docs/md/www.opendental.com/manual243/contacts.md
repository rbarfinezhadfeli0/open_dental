Contacts

The Contacts lists is a reference list of phone and fax numbers you commonly use.

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Lists, Contacts.

![](images/contacts.png)

Alternatively, press **Ctrl**+**Shift**+**C**.

See [Definitions: Contact Categories](definitionscontactcategories.html) to customize the Category list.

Click **Add** to create a new contact or double-click an existing contact to edit.

![](images/contactEdit.png)

Click **Delete** to remove a contact from the list.

Enter details and click **Save** to finish adding the contact or keep changes.
