LOINC Codes

LOINC codes (Logical Observation Identifiers Names and Codes) are a standard for identifying medical laboratory observations.

To import LOINC codes, see [Importing Code Systems](codesystemimport.html).

LOINC codes (Logical Observation Identifiers Names and Codes) is a standard for identifying medical laboratory observations. They are used in CQMs, laboratory and radiology orders, clinical summaries, summaries of care, and when validating syndromic surveillance and vaccine data. LOINC codes are only available for United States customers.

To view imported LOINC codes, click **LOINC** on the [EHR Setup Window](ehrsetupwindow.html).

**Code or Description**: Enter characters to filter the list, then click **Search**. Select a code and click **OK**.
