NewCrop eRx Doctor's List

Ensora eRx (formerly NewCrop eRx) users can create a list of frequently prescribed drugs by adding to Favorites.

In the [Ensora eRX / Prescription](newcrop.html) interface, click **Favorites**.

## Adding a Favorite Prescription from List Maintenance

1. From the Compose Rx tab, click the Favorites dropdown. Click **Add Favorite**.
   1. Alternatively, click the **Admin** tab in the top toolbar.
   2. Click **Doctor's Favorites** in the *List Maintenance* area.
2. Enter search criteria for the drug and click **Drug Search**.
![](images/newCropAddFavorite.png)3. Click on the drug and dosage combination.
4. Enter the Sig information
5. Click **Save**.

## Adding a Favorite Prescription while Prescribing

When prescribing:

1. In the Edit Rx window **Add to Favorites**.
2. Click **Prescribe**.

The prescription along with any entered Sig information is saved to the Doctor's Favorites list.

![](images/newCropAddFavoriteRx.png)

## Copying a Doctor's Favorites List

If a prescriber has saved favorites, another prescriber can copy all of the saved favorites into their own list.

To copy a doctor's favorites list:

1. Go to the **Admin** Tab in Ensora eRx.
2. Click **Doctor's Favorites** in the *List Maintenance* area.
3. At the top is a *Favorites List Copy* tool.
![](images/newCropFavoritesCopy.png)4. Select the *Copy From* prescriber in the left dropdown.
5. Select the doctor's name to *Copy To* from the right dropdown. Type in the box to narrow results.
6. Click **Copy**.
7. "Favorites List Copied!" confirmation is shown once the copy is complete.

Copied favorites are added to any existing favorites already in the Doctor's Favorites list.

## Deleting Favorites

To delete drugs from a Doctor's Favorites list:

1. Go to the **Admin** Tab in Ensora eRx.
2. Click **Doctor's Favorites** in the *List Maintenance* area.
3. At the bottom, in the *Favorite List* area, check the drug(s) to delete.
4. Click **Delete**.
![](images/newCropFavoireDelete.png)5. A confirmation appears. Click **Yes, remove** to confirm the deletion. Click **Cancel** to stop the deletion.
