Edit Patient Information: Emergency Contact Tab

Note a patient's emergency contact information to display in the Family Module.

In the [Edit Patient Information](patientedit.html) window, click the Emergency Contact tab.

![](images/patientEditEmergency.png)

**Emergency Name**: The emergency contact's name.

**Emergency Phone**: The emergency contact's phone number.

Affects the information shown in the *ICE Name* and *ICE Phone* [Display Fields](displayfields.html) of the [Family Module](family.html), Patient Information grid.
