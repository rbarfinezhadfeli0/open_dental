Registration Forms

See [Patient Forms](patientform.html).

Registration Forms are the paperwork patients complete when they first become patients, such as a new patient registration form, medical history, HIPAA form, financial agreement, etc.

## Filling Out Forms

There are several ways a patient can fill out registration forms.

**Paper Forms**: Patients fill out paper forms in your office. Staff enters data into the Open Dental Database, then scans the original documents into the Imaging Module. The originals can be shredded because no paper chart is needed.

**Kiosk**: Patients fill out forms on a [Kiosk](kioskpatient.html). Then staff can import the data into the database.

**Web Forms**: Before coming in, patients fill out forms online via [Web Forms](../site/webforms.html). Staff retrieves completed forms, then can import the data into the database.

**eClipboard**: Patients fill out forms on a tablet using [eClipboard](../site/eclipboard.html). Then staff can import the data into the database.

## Paper Form Templates
