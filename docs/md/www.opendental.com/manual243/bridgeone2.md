One2 Bridge

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Setup, [Program Links](programlinks.html). Double-click **One2 from https://osstem.com/**.

![](images/bridgeOne2.png)

One2 is an imaging software. Website: [www.osstem.com](https://osstem.com/).

To enable the bridge:

1. Check the **Enabled** box.
2. Verify the **Path of file to open**.
3. Double-click on a row in **Additional Properties** to change settings.
   * Use the PatNum (enter 0) or ChartNum (enter 1) as the patient ID.
   * Verify the **Patient.ini** path.
4. Set up a clickable bridge button.
   1. Under **Add a button to these toolbars**, highlight where to display the button.
   2. Enter the **Text on button**.
   3. A Button Image is automatically added and is visible in the toolbar once enabled.
5. Click Save.

## Technical Details

Patients must have a first name, gender, and birthdate entered for the bridge to launch.

If the patient does not exist in One2, a patient is created.
