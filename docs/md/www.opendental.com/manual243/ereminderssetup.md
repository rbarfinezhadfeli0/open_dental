eReminders

eReminders are an eService that sends automated text messages and/or email reminders about upcoming appointments.

In [eServices Setup](eservicessetup.html), click [Automated Messaging](automatedmessaging.html).

![](images/eConfirmationsActivate.png)

eReminders are intended to be sent a short time before an appointment so patients are reminded of their appointment.

* ![](images/YouTube_PlayButton_15px.png)See our video: [eServices: Automated Messaging](https://youtu.be/YuKNAHdNC9g?si=MBra72MmUurdvahW).
* Emailed eReminders can be sent at no additional cost. To send eReminders by text message, Integrated Text Messaging is required and a per text message fee applies. See [eServices Signup](eservicessignup.html).
* Also see [eReminder and eConfirmation Troubleshooting](eremindertroubleshooting.html).

## Activate / Deactivate eReminders

eReminders can be activated or deactivated as needed.

![](images/eReminderActivate.png)

* To enable eReminders, click **Activate eReminders**. The eReminder row displays in green with a status of *Active*.
* To disable eReminders, click **Deactivate eReminders**. The eReminder row displays in red with a status of *Inactive*.

## eReminder Rules

To edit an existing eReminder Rule, double-click a Reminder Rule from the Automated Messaging Rules grid. To add a new rule, click **Add eReminder**.

For information on adding and editing rules, see [Edit Rule](editrule.html).
