Telephone Numbers

Use the Telephone Numbers tools to reformat numbers.

In the [Main Menu](mainmenu.html), click Tools, Misc Tools, **Telephone Numbers**.

![](images/telephoneNumbers.png)

This tool reformats telephone numbers in the following places:

* Patient phone numbers
* ICE phone numbers on patient notes
* Carrier phone numbers

Click **Reformat**.
