ODTouch: Perio

View and create perio charts for the patient.

From the ODTouch: Home Screen, tap Perio.

This screen is not available when using a mobile phone device.

All perio exams for the patient are listed. Tap the slider (two vertical lines) on an exam to delete or copy it.

Tap an exam to open it, or tap + to create a new exam.

Use the keypad to enter measurements. Tap the caret to view additional options:

  • Voice Command: Tap to use voice commands to enter perio measurements. See Voice Perio Charting for a list of available commands.
  • More Options: Tap to view additional options.
    • Settings: Determine different perio charting settings. For details on settings, see Perio Chart.
    • Summary: View a read-only summary of the perio chart. In the Summary screen, tap the caret to view a count of various measurements.
    • Graphical: View a graphical summary of the perio chart.
