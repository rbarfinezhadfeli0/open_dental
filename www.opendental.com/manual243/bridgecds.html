Central Data Storage CDS Bridge

In the Main Menu, click Setup, Program Links. Double-click on Central Data Storage.

Central Data Storage (CDS) is off-site STaaS (storage as a service). To sign up and begin using their services, visit their website: Central Data Storage. Also see Online Backups.

The only purpose of the CDS bridge is to remove the CDS button from the Backup window.

  1. Verify that Enabled is NOT checked.
  2. Check Hide Unused Button.
  3. Click Save.
