Apex

Apex is an E-Claims clearinghouse.

In Clearinghouses, double-click Apex.

Apex accepts both dental and medical claims. Contact Apex for details: http://www.apexedi.com

Setup

Default settings are preset in this window. Verify settings before sending claims.

Insurance and Claims

Contact Apex for additional required steps when sending claims electronically using Apex.

If claims are sent as a batch to Apex, preauthorizations and claims must be sent separately (Send Claims). If sent together, Apex merges the data and rejects the preauthorizations for having a date of service.
