XCharge Add Card

For practices utilizing XCharge, cards can be saved to patient accounts without processing a payment.

In Credit Card Manage, click Add.

The X-Charge Archive Vault window will launch.

Enter the credit or debit card information and click OK.

Credit/debit entries are automatically stored when processing a payment and Save Token is checked.

A token is now created for the card, masking the first 12 digits of the card number. The card will now list in the Credit Card Manage window and will be an available payment option in the Payment window.
