Resources for Current Users

Access to our comprehensive documentation is limited to customers on Support.

To access our documentation, launch Open Dental on your workstation. From any window, click the question mark icon (?) in the upper right. This will launch the corresponding Help Page and provide access to all available Help pages.

Resources for Potential and Former Users

If you are considering Open Dental, or your office was formerly on Support, much of our documentation is private.

For a brief overview of our features, browse our public Quick Manual.
