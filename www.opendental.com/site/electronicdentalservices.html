Electronic Dental Services (EDS)

Electronic Dental Services (EDS) is a clearinghouse available in Open Dental.

EDS can be used within Open Dental for the following services:

  • Electronic Claims: EDS can be used for dental and medical claims
  • Electronic Claim Attachments
  • Electronic Billing
  • Electronic Remittance Advice (ERA)
  • Electronic Eligibility and Benefits

Getting Started

Contact EDS to sign up: edsedi.com. EDS will supply the program and setup.

Using EDS

Quickly process eligibility and benefits requests in Open Dental to ensure the accuracy of patient insurance benefits. Efficiently import benefits right to an insurance plan

Easily send electronic claims right from Open Dental using EDS. Individual patient claims or claim batches can be sent.

Electronic attachments can be easily uploaded to claims from Open Dental.

Send bills electronically using EDS right from the Billing List.

Receive Electronic Remittance Advice directly in Open Dental from EDS once you've enrolled with carriers. ERAs can even be automatically downloaded and processed to streamline workflow.
